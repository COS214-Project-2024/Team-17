\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
Represents a community center landmark building. 

The \mbox{\hyperlink{classLandCCenter}{Land\+CCenter}} class inherits from the \mbox{\hyperlink{classLandmark}{Landmark}} class and provides specific implementations for managing visitors, resources, and employee interactions at a community center.
