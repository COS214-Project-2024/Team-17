\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
Represents a composite road component consisting of multiple road sections. 

The \mbox{\hyperlink{classRoadsComposite}{Roads\+Composite}} class allows for the management of a collection of \mbox{\hyperlink{classRoadComponent}{Road\+Component}} objects, enabling operations like adding, removing, and calculating traffic for a composite road section.
