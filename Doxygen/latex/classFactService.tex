\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
Represents a factory that creates various service buildings. 

The \mbox{\hyperlink{classFactService}{Fact\+Service}} class inherits from the \mbox{\hyperlink{classFactoryBuilding}{Factory\+Building}} class and implements methods for creating different types of service buildings such as educational, security, and entertainment services, as well as residential, commercial, industrial, and landmark buildings.
