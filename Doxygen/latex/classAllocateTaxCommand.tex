\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
A command to allocate a specific amount of tax to a government department. 

This class inherits from \mbox{\hyperlink{classTaxCommand}{Tax\+Command}} and is responsible for executing an allocation of tax funds to a specified department within a government entity.
