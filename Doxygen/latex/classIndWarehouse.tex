\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
Represents an industrial warehouse building. 

The \mbox{\hyperlink{classIndWarehouse}{Ind\+Warehouse}} class inherits from the \mbox{\hyperlink{classIndustrial}{Industrial}} class and provides specific implementations for managing production capacity, resources, and employee interactions at a warehouse facility.
