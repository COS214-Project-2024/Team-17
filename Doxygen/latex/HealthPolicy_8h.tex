\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
Header file for the \mbox{\hyperlink{classHealthPolicy}{Health\+Policy}} class, defining health-\/oriented budget allocation. 

This file defines the \mbox{\hyperlink{classHealthPolicy}{Health\+Policy}} class, which implements a budget allocation strategy focused on health. The policy allocates a significant portion of the budget to health, with remaining funds divided among education and infrastructure. \mbox{\hyperlink{classHealthPolicy}{Health\+Policy}} integrates with the \mbox{\hyperlink{classCityStructure}{City\+Structure}} to apply its unique budgeting approach in a health-\/prioritized manner. \begin{DoxyNote}{Note}
\mbox{\hyperlink{classHealthPolicy}{Health\+Policy}} serves as one of several budget policies available to the \mbox{\hyperlink{classGovernment}{Government}} class for city resource distribution. 
\end{DoxyNote}
