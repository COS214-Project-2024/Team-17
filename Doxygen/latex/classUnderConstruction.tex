\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
Represents a road state indicating that the road is under construction. 

The \mbox{\hyperlink{classUnderConstruction}{Under\+Construction}} class implements the \mbox{\hyperlink{classRoadState}{Road\+State}} interface, providing specific behavior for handling traffic when a road is currently under construction.
