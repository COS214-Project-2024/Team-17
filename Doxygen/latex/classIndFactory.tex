\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
Represents an industrial factory building. 

The \mbox{\hyperlink{classIndFactory}{Ind\+Factory}} class inherits from the \mbox{\hyperlink{classIndustrial}{Industrial}} class and implements the functionality specific to an industrial factory, including production capacity, employee management, and utility management.
