\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
Represents a citizen in the city simulation. 

The \mbox{\hyperlink{classCitizen}{Citizen}} class extends \mbox{\hyperlink{classCityBlock}{City\+Block}} and encapsulates the properties and behaviors of a citizen, including their state, location, workplace, and activities.
