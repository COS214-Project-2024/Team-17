\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
Represents a bus that transports citizens between locations. 

The \mbox{\hyperlink{classBus}{Bus}} class extends the \mbox{\hyperlink{classCitizen}{Citizen}} class and manages a list of passengers, their destinations, and the route taken by the bus.
